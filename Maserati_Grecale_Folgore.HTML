Maserati Grecale Folgore

Overview:

The Maserati Grecale Folgore represents Maserati's bold leap into electrification, combining Italian luxury with zero-emission performance. As the all-electric variant of the Grecale SUV, the Folgore ("Lightning" in Italian) delivers instant torque and silent power with unmistakable Maserati styling. The signature Trident grille evolves with a backlit design, while copper accents distinguish this electric model. With a perfect 50/50 weight distribution and whisper-quiet operation, the Folgore redefines what an electric luxury SUV can be.

Technical Specifications:

  • Dual electric motors (AWD) with 550 horsepower
  • 800 Nm of instant torque available from 0 rpm
  • 0-100 km/h in 4.1 seconds
  • 105 kWh battery with 400V architecture
  • Estimated range of 500 km (WLTP)
  • DC fast charging up to 150 kW (20-80% in 29 minutes)
  • Weight distribution: 50% front / 50% rear

Interior & Features:

  • Digital cockpit with 12.3" cluster and 12.3" infotainment
  • Additional 8.8" comfort display for climate controls
  • Premium recycled materials with copper accent stitching
  • Sonus Faber 3D sound system with EV-specific tuning
  • Active noise cancellation technology
  • Full-length panoramic glass roof
  • Maserati Intelligent Assistant (MIA) with natural voice control

Driving Experience:

The Grecale Folgore delivers a uniquely Maserati electric experience. The instant torque provides explosive acceleration without hesitation, while the low center of gravity from the floor-mounted battery gives it sports car-like handling. Three regenerative braking levels (including one-pedal driving mode) allow for customizable energy recovery. Despite its electric nature, Maserati engineers have created an artificial sound signature that evolves with speed - a futuristic yet emotional soundtrack worthy of the Trident badge.

Safety & Technology:

  • Level 2 autonomous driving capabilities
  • 360-degree camera with transparent bonnet view
  • Predictive energy management with route planning
  • Battery preconditioning for optimal charging
  • Advanced thermal management system
  • 8 airbags and reinforced battery protection

Why Choose the Grecale Folgore?

The Grecale Folgore isn't just an electric SUV - it's a Maserati first. It preserves the brand's soul while embracing the future, offering the perfect balance between eco-consciousness and driving emotion. Unlike many electric vehicles that prioritize efficiency over excitement, the Folgore delivers genuine performance with a luxurious Italian flair. The exclusive copper accents, EV-specific wheel designs, and backlit Trident logo make a subtle yet distinctive statement. For those who want to make the electric transition without compromising on style, luxury, or the thrill of driving, the Grecale Folgore stands as the most compelling choice in its class.
