BENTLEY GT S

Overview:

The BENTLEY GT S is a high-performance, luxury grand tourer that blends British craftsmanship with sporty dynamics and a bold design. It's a sportier variant of the standard Continental GT, tailored for drivers who want a more engaging and athletic driving experience without compromising on comfort and opulence.

Technical Specifications:

  • Engine Type: 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8
  • Power Output: 542 hp (550 PS)
  • Torque: 770 Nm (568 lb-ft)
  • AMG SPEEDSHIFT TCT 9-speed automatic transmission
  • Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • Drivetrain: Active All-Wheel Drive
  • 0 – 100 km/h (0 – 62 mph): Approx. 4.0 seconds Top Speed: 318 km/h (198 mph)

Interior & Features:

  • Luxury Sporty Design: Premium leather with Dinamica suede, “S” embroidery, and bold color options.
  • otating Display (Optional): 12.3” touchscreen Classic analog dials Seamless wood veneer
  • udio Systems: Standard: Bentley Audio Optional: Bang & Olufsen or Naim (high-performance)
  • Comfort: Front seats: Heated, ventilated, massaging, fully electric Rear seats: Stylish but limited legroom
  • Technology: Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, Navigation Digital cockpit + Head-Up Display
  • Driver Assistance: 360° cameras, lane assist, adaptive cruise control, optional night vision
  • Driving Experience: Multiple drive modes, enhanced Sport Mode, sporty exhaust sound

Driving Experience:

Sport-Tuned V8: 4.0L twin-turbo V8 – responsive, powerful (542 hp), with thrilling acceleration. Sport Mode Focused: Sharper throttle, tighter suspension, quicker gear shifts, and a louder, more aggressive exhaust sound. Active All-Wheel Drive: Delivers excellent grip, especially in corners or wet conditions. Bentley Dynamic Ride: 48V active anti-roll control system keeps the car stable and flat during fast cornering. Air Suspension: Adaptive – smooth when cruising, firm when sporty driving is needed. Steering Feel: Light and easy at low speeds, more precise and connected at higher speeds. Overall Feel: A perfect mix of grand touring comfort and sporty performance — ideal for long-distance drives and spirited weekend fun.

Safety & Technology:

  • Adaptive Cruise Control: Maintains a safe distance from other vehicles, adjusting speed automatically.
  • Lane Assist & Lane Departure Warning: Alerts you if you unintentionally drift out of your lane.
  • Traffic Sign Recognition: Identifies road signs (like speed limits) and displays them on the screen.
  • Night Vision (Optional): Uses infrared to help you see pedestrians and animals in low-light conditions.
  • Surround View Camera: 360° camera system to assist with parking and low-speed maneuvers.

Why Choose the Bentley GT S?

Powerful Performance: 542 hp V8 engine with sharp handling and sport-tuned suspension for an exciting driving experience. Luxury & Comfort: Premium materials, customizable interiors, and advanced features like massage and heated seats for ultimate comfort. Cutting-Edge Technology: Features like Apple CarPlay, 360° cameras, adaptive cruise control, and an optional Naim sound system enhance both safety and convenience. Distinctive Design: Sporty yet elegant, with bold styling and personalization options to make it unique. Exclusivity & Prestige: A Bentley is a symbol of luxury, craftsmanship, and exclusivity, offering a rare and sophisticated driving experience.
