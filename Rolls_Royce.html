With its commanding presence, iconic Rolls-Royce design elements, and an interior that envelops passengers in pure opulence, the Cullinan stands as a masterpiece on wheels. Technical Specifications:

  • 6.75L V12 twin-turbocharged engine for effortless power delivery
  • Produces 563 horsepower and 850 Nm of torque
  • Acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h in approximately 5.2 seconds
  • 8-speed automatic transmission with satellite-aided shift technology
  • All-wheel drive and all-wheel steering for supreme control and agility
  • Self-leveling air suspension for an unmatched "Magic Carpet Ride" experience

Interior & Features:

  • Wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Premium Burmester® 3D surround sound system
  • Panoramic sliding sunroof and illuminated AMG door sills
