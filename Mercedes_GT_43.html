Mercedes Gt 43

Overview:

The Mercedes Gt 43 is where elegance meets adrenaline. It's a luxury sports coupe engineered for those who demand both aesthetic perfection and uncompromising performance. With a bold and aggressive exterior design, signature Gt 43 styling, and a meticulously crafted interior, the Gt 43 sets a new benchmark in its class. Whether you're cruising down the coastline or navigating the city at night, this masterpiece delivers a commanding presence and an exhilarating drive every time.

Technical Specifications:

  • 3.0L inline-6 turbocharged engine with integrated EQ Boost mild-hybrid system
  • Delivers up to 443 horsepower and 560 Nm of torque
  • Acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h in just 4.2 seconds
  • Gt 43 SPEEDSHIFT TCT 9-speed automatic transmission
  • Gt 43 Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive system for maximum grip
  • Gt 43 adaptive sport suspension with multiple driving modes
  • Top speed electronically limited to 250 km/h (can be increased with Gt 43 Driver’s Package)

Interior & Features:

  • Luxurious Gt 43 leather performance seats with red contrast stitching
  • Dual widescreen cockpit with 12.3” digital cluster + 11.9” central touchscreen
  • Ambient lighting with 64 customizable colors
  • Multicontour massage seats with heating & ventilation
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Premium Burmester® 3D surround sound system
  • Panoramic sliding sunroof and illuminated Gt 43 door sills

Driving Experience:

The Gt 43 delivers a dynamic driving experience tailored to your mood. Engage Comfort mode for a smooth, quiet cruise, or switch to Sport+ to unleash aggressive exhaust notes and razor-sharp throttle response. The Gt 43 performance exhaust roars with authority, while the adaptive damping system offers precision handling in every curve. It’s not just a car — it’s a symphony of engineering and emotion on wheels.

Safety & Technology:

  • Active Distance Assist DISTRONIC (adaptive cruise control)
  • Lane Keeping Assist & Blind Spot Monitoring
  • 360-degree camera with active parking assist
  • Active Brake Assist with cross-traffic function
  • PRE-SAFE® system for collision preparation
