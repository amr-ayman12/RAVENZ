BMW M4 Competition

Overview:

The BMW M4 Competition is a high-performance sports coupe that redefines power, precision, and presence. With its bold kidney grille, muscular stance, and aerodynamic design, the M4 is built to dominate the road and the track. Whether you're seeking daily luxury or weekend thrills, the M4 delivers unmatched versatility, wrapped in unmistakable M styling and motorsport heritage.

Technical Specifications:

  • 3.0L BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline-6 engine
  • Delivers 503 horsepower and 650 Nm of torque
  • 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.9 seconds
  • 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission
  • Available with rear-wheel drive or M xDrive all-wheel drive
  • Adaptive M suspension and active M differential
  • Electronically limited top speed of 250 km/h (290 km/h with M Driver’s Package)

Interior & Features:

  • M Carbon bucket seats with illuminated M logos
  • 12.3” digital instrument cluster and 14.9” curved infotainment screen
  • BMW iDrive 8 system with gesture control
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Harman Kardon surround sound system
  • Ambient lighting and M-specific interior trims
  • Three-zone automatic climate control

Driving Experience:

The BMW M4 offers thrilling performance with everyday usability. Whether in Comfort mode or Sport Plus, it reacts instantly to your commands. The precise steering, dynamic chassis, and customizable drive settings make it feel like a true driver’s machine. From canyon curves to autobahn blasts, the M4 is confidence-inspiring and exhilarating at every speed.

Safety & Technology:

  • Driving Assistant Professional with lane keeping and traffic jam assist
  • Adaptive cruise control with stop & go function
  • Parking Assistant Plus with 360-degree camera
  • Frontal collision warning with city collision mitigation
  • Cross-traffic alert and blind-spot detection

Why Choose the BMW M4?

The BMW M4 isn’t just a coupe — it’s a symbol of dynamic excellence, German precision, and bold personality. Built for those who demand excitement without compromising comfort or innovation, the M4 delivers adrenaline in every gear. Its legacy is forged on racetracks, and its comfort is refined for city streets. With cutting-edge technology, thrilling performance, and an aggressive design that commands respect, the M4 is perfect for enthusiasts who want it all — speed, style, and status. Driving the M4 is more than a choice — it's a statement.
