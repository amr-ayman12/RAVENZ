BMW M5 CS

Overview:

The BMW M5 CS is a high-performance, limited-edition sports sedan that takes the legendary M5 to new extremes. Built with lightweight materials and enhanced M engineering, the M5 CS blends track-ready performance with everyday usability. From its Gold Bronze accents to its aggressive stance and carbon fiber details, it’s a bold statement of power, precision, and exclusivity.

Technical Specifications:

  • 4.4L BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine
  • Delivers 627 horsepower and 750 Nm of torque
  • 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.0 seconds
  • 8-speed M Steptronic transmission with Drivelogic
  • M xDrive all-wheel drive with rear-wheel bias
  • Carbon ceramic brakes and adaptive M suspension
  • Top speed electronically limited to 305 km/h

Interior & Features:

  • Carbon fiber M bucket seats with illuminated M5 logos
  • Rear individual sport seats – four-seater layout
  • Alcantara-wrapped steering wheel with red center marker
  • 12.3” digital display and iDrive 7 touchscreen system
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Premium sound system and ambient lighting
  • Exclusive CS badging and lightweight trim pieces

Driving Experience:

The BMW M5 CS offers an electrifying drive — lightweight construction, upgraded suspension, and track-level tuning ensure incredible agility and response. It’s aggressive when you want it, yet refined when you need it. The V8 roar, precise steering, and race-bred dynamics make every road feel like your personal circuit.

Safety & Technology:

  • Driving Assistant Professional with lane centering and adaptive cruise
  • Active Blind Spot Detection and lane departure warning
  • 360-degree camera system and parking assistant
  • Frontal collision warning with braking intervention
  • Carbon fiber-reinforced safety structure

Why Choose the BMW M5 CS?

The BMW M5 CS is not just the fastest M car ever built — it’s a symbol of elite engineering and exclusivity. With production limited and performance enhanced, the M5 CS is for drivers who demand the extraordinary. Every detail is crafted with purpose, from its carbon fiber panels to its Gold Bronze accents. Whether tearing through corners or cruising city streets, the M5 CS blends brutality and sophistication in perfect harmony. If you're after a sedan with supercar spirit and track-proven DNA, the M5 CS delivers — and then some.
