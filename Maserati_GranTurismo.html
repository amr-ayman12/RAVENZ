Maserati GranTurismo

Overview:

The Maserati GranTurismo redefines the grand touring experience with its perfect blend of Italian luxury and exhilarating performance. This latest generation maintains the iconic long hood/short deck proportions that made the original legendary, now with sharper lines and modern aerodynamic enhancements.

Technical Specifications:

  • 3.0L twin-turbo V6 Nettuno engine
  • 490-550 hp output
  • 0-100 km/h in 3.5s
  • Top speed of 320 km/h
  • 8-speed dual-clutch transmission
  • Near-perfect 50/50 weight distribution

Interior & Features:

  • Hand-stitched Poltrona Frau leather
  • 12.2" digital instrument cluster
  • 12.3" central touchscreen
  • 21-speaker Sonus Faber audio
  • Heated/ventilated/massaging seats

Driving Experience:

Behind the wheel of the GranTurismo, every journey becomes an event. The V6 delivers a sonorous exhaust note that builds to a glorious crescendo. The adaptive suspension transitions from comfort to track-ready stiffness at the touch of a button, while the steering provides perfect feedback.

Safety & Technology:

  • Level 2 autonomous driving
  • 360-degree camera system
  • Predictive emergency braking
  • Active blind spot monitoring

Why Choose the GranTurismo?

The GranTurismo represents the purest expression of Maserati's dual identity - both a luxury tourer and performance machine. The cabin wraps occupants in exquisite materials while the powertrain delivers racetrack-worthy thrills. With timeless design and perfect balance, it's automotive art in motion.
