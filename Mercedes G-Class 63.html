Mercedes G-Class 63

Overview:

The Mercedes G-Class 63 is the high-performance version of the iconic G-Class SUV, blending off-road ruggedness with luxury and raw power. It’s known for its boxy design, powerful V8 engine, and ultra-luxurious features, making it one of the most desirable luxury SUVs in the world.

Technical Specifications:

  • Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo (M177)
  • Power: 577 hp (430 kW)
  • Torque: 850 Nm (627 lb-ft)
  • 0–100 km/h: ~4.5 sec Top Speed: 220–240 km/h (137–149 mph)
  • Transmission: 9-speed G-Class 63 SPEEDSHIFT
  • G-Class 63 adaptive sport suspension with multiple driving modes
  • Drive: All-Wheel Drive (40:60 rear bias)

Interior & Features:

  • Premium Nappa Leather Upholstery – Optional diamond stitching and custom color themes
  • Heated, Ventilated, and Massaging Seats (Front and Rear)
  • Multicontour Front Seats with memory function
  • G-Class 63 Performance Steering Wheel in Nappa leather or DINAMICA microfiber
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Premium Burmester® 3D surround sound system 64-Color Ambient Lighting
  • Panoramic sliding sunroof and illuminated G-Class 63 door sills

Driving Experience:

The G-Class 63 performance exhaust roars with authority, while the adaptive damping system offers precision handling in every curve. It’s not just a car — it’s a symphony of engineering and emotion on wheels.

Safety & Technology:

  • Active Brake Assist Detects obstacles, vehicles, and pedestrians; can apply autonomous emergency braking.
  • Traffic Sign Assist Recognizes and displays traffic signs (speed limits, no entry, etc.) on the digital cluster.
  • 360-degree camera with active parking assist
  • Active Brake Assist with cross-traffic function
  • PRE-SAFE® system for collision preparation
