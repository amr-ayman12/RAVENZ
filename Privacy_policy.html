Privacy Policy

The following privacy policy offers you an overview of which personal data we as Motor Match collect during your visit to our website and how it is used.

At our car showroom, we deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. When you visit our showroom or interact with our staff, we may collect data such as your name, contact details, and vehicle preferences to assist you with your inquiries and provide tailored services. Be assured that this information is handled with the utmost confidentiality and is solely used for legitimate business purposes, such as facilitating test drives, providing vehicle information, and processing potential purchases. We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Our detailed privacy policy, outlining our data collection practices, your rights, and how we safeguard your information, is readily available upon request and on our website for your review, ensuring transparency and building trust in our commitment to your privacy.

    Types of Information Collected:

  • Contact Information: Name, address, email, phone number for communication and service updates.

  • Vehicle Preferences: Details about the type, make, model, and features of vehicles you're interested in.

  • Financial Information: Necessary for financing and credit checks, including income, employment, and banking details.

  • Trade-in Information: If you’re trading in a vehicle, we collect its make, model, year, VIN, mileage, and condition.

  • Showroom Visit Information: We may use security cameras and visitor logs for security and service improvement.

  • Marketing Preferences: Your choices about how we communicate with you (email, SMS, phone calls, etc.).

Importance of Reviewing the Policy:

We encourage all visitors to carefully review this Privacy Policy before sharing any personal data. Understanding our practices helps you make informed decisions about your privacy.
